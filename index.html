Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does funeral cover include? It covers funeral expenses like burial, coffin, transport, and service fees.
  • Who can I add to my policy? Spouse, children, parents, in-laws, and extended family.
  • When can I claim? Claims can be submitted as soon as the policyholder passes away and documentation is ready.
  • How long is the waiting period? 6 months for natural death, immediate cover for accidental death.
  • What happens if I skip a payment? A grace period applies, but long non-payment may cancel the policy.
